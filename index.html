About PixelPlate

Our purpose

PixelPlate exists to bring game worlds to life at the dinner table. Every restaurant is designed as a themed experience.

Who it's for

  • Families looking for a fun, memorable night out.
  • Couples wanting a themed date around a shared favourite.
  • Fans visiting iconic locations from their favourite games.

What we offer

Restaurant Discovery Browse game-themed venues, filter by world, cuisine and price.
Smart Recommendations Answer a few questions, get personalised restaurant matches.
Easy Reservations Secure bookings with a transparent deposit — no surprises.
